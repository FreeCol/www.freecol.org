Project member leaving
Monday, 11 September 2006
Lars Willemsens, the first leader of our development team, has now left the project. He did a great job at managing the project and was also the lead programmer in the first two years. Thank you for your work on our promising little project. :-)

The continuation of development is assured by both old and new developers, some of which have joined in the past few months.
